So in order to hold myself accountable, I've been logging my daily grind in a notebook for the past few months. I figured it was past time to convert that to something digital. Most of what you will see here is barebones, bulletpoint summaries of what I spent hours studying. I may make an effort to flesh out future posts, but for now, here is my daily coding blog.
